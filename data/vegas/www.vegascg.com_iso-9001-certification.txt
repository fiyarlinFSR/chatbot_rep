FAQ







 What Is an ISO 9001 Quality Management System Certification? 



ISO 9001 Quality Management System (QMS) certification provided by external certification bodies to the companies that meets a certain set of criteria and international standard.





 How to Get ISO 9001 Certification in (Location)? 



In order to get certified at your location follows the steps as mentioned.

Decide to get certified
Appoint a senior for internal management
Perform gap analysis
Create a plan
Improve, implement and develop the quality system
Review the system
Choose a certifier
Perform external audits
Get certified





 Why ISO 9001 Certification Is Important? 



An ISO 9001 Certification is required for many developing companies to improve their image and credibility. It is also a good marketing tool, which also increases the quality value of products and services.





 What Are the Benefits of a QMS? 



The main benefits of implementing QMS in a company is to provide focus on key areas of the business the company is involved in and to improve its efficiency over a period of time.

When a company gets certified it improves its customer satisfaction, reduce real-time mistakes, and achieve consistency in production of products.





 What Is Quality Environmental and Health Safety Certificate? 



This Certificate is important for any organization in order to tell your personnel and customers that your company invests in creating a safe workplace for its employees, consultants, and other personnel.





 What Is the Meaning of QMS? 



A QMS is a regulated system of document processes, procedures or a framework of business based process focused on satisfying customer requirements.





 What Is the Purpose of a Quality Management System? 



The main purpose of QMS is to drive an organization into long term success. It is also to ensure that produced products adhere to the defined quality standard.





 What Are ISO 9001:2015 Requirements? 



The ISO 9001:2015 certification within an organization not only includes customer needs but also some of the following requirements.

Determining internal and external issues
Leadership and commitment
Planning to address Risks
Providing resource needs
Plan and process control
Monitoring QMS
Taking Action





 What Are ISO 9001:2015 Clauses? 



The clauses of 9001: 2015 are as follows:

1. Scope

2. Normative reference

3. Terms and Definitions

4. Context of the organization

5. Leadership

6. Planning

7. Support

8. Operation

9. Performance Evaluation

10. Improvement
