ISO 50001:2015 Certification
ISO 50001:2015 certification specifies requirements to set up an Energy Management System (EMS) that enables an organization to develop and implement a policy and control for identifying and maintaining significant areas of energy consumption and reduction. It determines the best practices in energy management through validating existing national standards and initiatives.
The certification requires organizations to promote energy savings initiatives and establishes for the reduction of energy intensiveness of a company’s operation.
Implementing ISO 50001 provides a framework for establishing a power system or energy management to help organizations to improve their energy efficiency. The standard requires the establishment of processes and systems that are needed to improve energy performance, including energy efficiency, use, and consumption.
ISO 50001 certification demonstrates an organization’s commitment to continual improvement in energy management and encourages the more effective use of your resources. Vegas Consulting provides accelerated learning techniques to make sure your organization gains maximum benefit from this energy management standard.
Certifying to ISO 50001 to reduce costs, comply with legislation, increase your sustainability, implementing the standard provides a systematic process approach to achieving all these benefits.








Principles Of ISO 50001

Some Of The Principles Of ISO 5001 Are
Providing A Design Based On A Structure Of Requirements That Gives The Result With Decrease In The Energy Intensity Of The Organization And Continuous Increase In Its Energy Efficiency.
Improving The Consumption Monitoring During All Activities And Confirmation Of Energy Utilizability And Consumption Limits For The Most Important Energy Applications.




Benefits

Organized Approach To Identify, Measure And Manage Energy Consumption
Enable Organizations To Reduce Greenhouse Gases (GHG) Emissions
Improved Image Of Organization With Customers, Partners, And The Public
Comply With Statutory And Regulatory Requirements In The Audit Report
Increasing The Consciousness For Implementing The Energy Management Policy And In Embedding Energy Efficient Thinking In Your Organization

FAQ







 Why Implement ISO 50001? 



This standard is applicable to both large and small organizations, irrespective of any conditions. It has been structured to be aligned with other popular industry management system standards, so it is possible to integrate an energy management system (EnMS) with your existing management system.





 How To Implement ISO 50001? 



Get commitment and support from senior management
Engage everyone in the business
Compare the requirements
Get feedbacks on current energy management
Establish an implementation team
Mapping the roles, responsibilities, and timescales
Adapting to ISO 50001 standard basic principles
Motivate staff involvement
Encourage staff to train as internal auditors
Review the system





 What Is EMS In Electrical? 



Operators of electric utility use the energy management system (EMS) through a system of computer-aided tools to monitor, control, and optimize the performance of the generation and/or transmission system.
