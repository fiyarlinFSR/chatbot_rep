AUDIENCE
Who can take this Practitioner Training Program?
The API Spec Q2 Practitioner training Course is specifically designed for a diverse range of professionals within the oil and gas industry, including:



API Monogram licensees and API applicants
Applications for APIQR Q2 registration
Quality assurance personnel and management representatives
Oil and gas service providers
Quality system auditors
Organization or personnel involved in oil and gas service activities like well construction, intervention, production and abandonment
Operators involved in upstream development
Service providers involved in upstream activities


Overall, this course caters to a wide audience involved in various aspects of quality management and certification within the oil and gas industry, offering valuable insights and knowledge tailored to their specific roles and responsibilities. 















Training Objectives and highlights 



          
         




Introduction to API Q2

Normative references

Contingency planning
Management of change (MOC)
