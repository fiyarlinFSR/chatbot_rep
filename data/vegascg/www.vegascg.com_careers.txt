1. QMS Consultant
We are looking for a QMS Consultant responsible for assisting senior consultants in implementing and maintaining effective quality management systems for clients in oil & Gas industries..

Experience: Fresher (Experience of 1 year in QMS preferable)
Job Location: Work from home

Key Responsibilities



Assist senior consultants in conducting assessments and audits of client organizations' existing quality management systems.





Collaborate with clients to understand their quality requirements, objectives, and industry-specific regulations

 


Assist in the design and implementation of metrics and key performance indicators (KPIs) to monitor quality performance and identify opportunities for improvement.





Support the creation and maintenance of quality documentation, including quality manuals, procedures, work instructions, and forms.





Analyse existing processes, procedures, and documentation to identify gaps and areas for improvement.





Stay updated with industry trends, regulations, and advancements in quality management practices.





Prepare reports and presentations to communicate findings, recommendations, and progress to cliens and senior consultants.





   
  Qualifications



Bachelor’s degree in mechanical engineering.





A relevant master's degree or certification in Quality Management Systems would be a plus.

 


Awareness on quality management principles or total quality management system





Awareness on inspection and testing process methodology




   
            Skill



Excellent communication and interpersonal skills to effectively interact with clients and team members.





Ability to work independently and collaboratively within a team environment.

 


Proficiency in using Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, PowerPoint) and other relevant software applications.





Flexibility to travel abroad for client sites as required.
