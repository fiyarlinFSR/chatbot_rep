AS9100D:2018 Certification
AS9100D:2018 specifies the requirement for the international management system standard in the Aircraft, Space, and Defense (AS&D) industry. It symbolizes the quality management or QMS for the aerospace industry. The standard provides suppliers with a complete quality system for providing safe and authentic products to the aerospace industry.
AS9100 / AS9100D certification can be achieved by all levels of the supply chain by organizations around the world to improve quality management, cost, and delivery performance. AS9100 quality management helps you optimize your organization’s supply chain and build mutually beneficial relationships with your suppliers.
Vegas Consulting is a market leader in AS9100 certification consulting, auditing, implementation, and training services for more than 10 years. We specialize in management systems compliance for the aerospace industry, including AS9100 gap analysis, and AS9100 assessment & certification.
Top most companies that are involved in this certification are companies involved in designing, developing and production of flight, space, and defense products.
The Aircraft, Space, and Defense (AS&D) industries needs AS9100 certified suppliers. This certification must be issued by Certification Bodies (CBs) recognized by the AS standards which are recognized by the International Aerospace Quality Group (IAQG).

AS9100D Certification
Aerospace Basic Quality System Standard
This standard which is ISO certified is related to the aerospace industry is the AS9001. This standard has its roots in the earlier version, the AS9000.
In view of the insufficiency of the ISO 9000; the ISO prepared an international standard that came to be called the AS9001. It was created to become a completely global standard in that it adapted the standards present in the aerospace industries in many countries.
Revisions to the AS9001
Till this year, AS9001 standard has undergone three revisions. These revisions for the standards were carried out in 2001, 2004 and 2009.

 AS9100D - Lead Auditor Training Course 



The 32-hour course is a subject-oriented training that provides training on the standard, but also on how to lead or conduct internal audits and supplier audits.





 Can a company hold both AS9100D and ISO 9001 certifications? 


Yes, a company can hold both AS9100D and ISO 9001 certifications. AS9100D includes all the requirements of ISO 9001, making it possible for an organization to comply with both standards simultaneously. Having both certifications can provide broader recognition and flexibility in serving clients from various industries.




Is AS9100D certification mandatory for aerospace companies? 


AS9100D certification is not mandatory, but it is often required or strongly recommended by major aerospace manufacturers and customers. Many aerospace companies seek AS9100D certification to meet industry expectations, enhance competitiveness, and gain access to a broader range of business opportunities within the aerospace sector.
